Who is a Maxxer?

A Maxxer is anyone who helps build, support, or grow the BinanceMaxxing ecosystem — from team members and degens to community users worldwide. Together, they embody the values that drive BinanceMaxxing forward:

Degen-First

Always put degens at the heart of every decision and action

Integrity

Build trust through honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct

Innovation

Pioneer new ideas that shape the future of crypto and memes

Teamwork

Collaborate with respect and unity to achieve moon goals

Commitment

Stay dedicated to BinanceMaxxing's mission and global community

Passion

Bring energy and belief in crypto's power to change the world

1. Get MetaMask

Download and install the MetaMask wallet extension for your browser or mobile device.
